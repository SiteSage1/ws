Tourist Destinations in Sri Lanka

Places that will capture your heart

Sigiriya

Ancient rock fortress and UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for its stunning frescoes and panoramic views.

Anuradhapura

One of the ancient capitals of Sri Lanka, famous for its magnificent stupas and sacred Bodhi tree.

Kandy

Cultural capital of Sri Lanka, home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth and scenic hill country.

Nuwara Eliya

Cool hill station known as "Little England" with tea plantations and colonial charm.

Galle

Historic Dutch fort and beautiful coastal city with a vibrant atmosphere.

Polonnaruwa

Ancient capital with well-preserved ruins and impressive Buddha statues.

Yala National Park

One of the best places for leopard safaris and wildlife viewing.

Ella

Scenic hill town famous for Nine Arch Bridge, waterfalls, and breathtaking views.

Adams Peak

Sacred mountain revered by multiple religions with the famous footprint.

Pinnawala

Famous elephant orphanage where you can see herds of elephants.

Horton Plains

Home to World's End with dramatic cliff views and beautiful landscapes.

Udawalawe National Park

Famous for large herds of elephants and excellent birdwatching.

Wilpattu National Park

Sri Lanka's largest national park, known for leopards and natural water basins.
